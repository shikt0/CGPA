About AIUB CGPA

AIUB follows a 4.0 GPA scale. Students can enter their marks and credits per course to calculate CGPA accurately, including retake marks. Fail warnings and earned credits are displayed automatically.

The responsive interface works on all devices. Semester data can be saved locally to continue tracking performance.

Letter grades appear next to marks for clarity. This premium SaaS-style calculator provides a modern and clean experience for AIUB students.

Regular use helps students monitor progress, plan semesters efficiently, and meet graduation requirements. SEO content ensures students can find it online easily.

FAQ

  • How is CGPA calculated at AIUB? Multiply each course credit by grade point, sum, divide by total credits.
  • Can retake marks be included? Yes, the calculator takes the higher grade automatically.
  • Does it show earned credits? Yes.
  • Can I save semester data? Yes, local browser storage.
  • How does fail warning work? Shows courses with F grade.
