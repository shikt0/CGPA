About East West University CGPA

East West University follows a 4.0 GPA scale. Students can enter marks and credits per course to calculate CGPA accurately, including retake marks. Fail warnings and earned credits are displayed automatically.

The interface is responsive for all devices and supports saving semester data in local storage.

Letter grades appear next to marks, making it easy for students to track progress. Premium SaaS-style design ensures a modern user experience.

Students can monitor performance, plan semesters efficiently, and meet graduation requirements. SEO content helps students easily find the calculator online.

FAQ

  • How is CGPA calculated? Multiply credit by grade point, sum all, divide by total credits.
  • Can retakes be entered? Yes, highest grade is considered automatically.
  • Does it show earned credits? Yes.
  • Can semester data be saved? Yes, locally.
  • Fail warnings? Courses with F are highlighted immediately.
