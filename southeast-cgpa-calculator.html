Southeast University CGPA Calculator – Bangladesh

This free Southeast University CGPA Calculator is built for students of Southeast University (SEU), one of Bangladesh's fastest-growing and most tech-forward private universities. It uses SEU's official grading scale to calculate your semester GPA and cumulative CGPA instantly and accurately.

How to Use This Calculator

  • Enter your course name (optional), credit hours, and marks in the fields above.
  • If you have a retake, enter those marks in the Retake column — the calculator uses them automatically.
  • Click Add Course to add more courses, or click Calculate CGPA directly.
  • Your CGPA, earned credits, and any failed course warnings appear instantly in the result section below.
  • Use Save Semester to store your course data in the browser for future reference.

SEU Grading System Explained

Southeast University uses a 10-level letter grade system ranging from A+ (4.00) for marks of 80–100, down to F (0.00 — Fail) for marks below 40. The minimum passing grade is D (2.00) for marks of 40–44. Any marks below 40 result in a Fail and the course must be retaken.

SEU is known for its emphasis on technology and innovation, offering programs in CSE, EEE, Business, Law, and many more. Failed courses count toward total attempted credits but receive zero grade points, which lowers your overall CGPA.
