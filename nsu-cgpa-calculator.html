NSU CGPA Calculator – North South University Bangladesh

This free NSU CGPA Calculator is built for students of North South University (NSU), one of Bangladesh's leading private universities. It uses NSU's official 4.0 grading scale to calculate your semester GPA and cumulative CGPA instantly and accurately.

How to Use This Calculator

  • Enter your course name (optional), credit hours, and marks in the fields above.
  • If you have a retake, enter those marks — the calculator uses them automatically.
  • Click Add Course for more courses, or hit Calculate CGPA directly.
  • Your CGPA, earned credits, and any fail warnings appear instantly in the result section.
  • Use Save Semester to store your data in the browser for later.

NSU Grading System Explained

NSU uses a letter grade system on a 4.0 scale. Marks of 93+ earn an A (4.00) while below 60 is an F (0.00 — Fail). Failed courses count as attempted credits but receive zero grade points, which lowers your CGPA. A CGPA of 3.70+ is Outstanding; 3.00–3.69 is Very Good to Excellent.

Frequently Asked Questions

NSU uses a 4.0 grading scale. Marks of 93–100 earn an A (4.00), and marks below 60 result in an F (0.00). Each letter grade maps to a specific grade point used in CGPA calculation.

NSU CGPA = Sum of (Grade Point × Credit Hours) ÷ Total Credit Hours Attempted. Failed courses receive 0.00 grade points but still count as attempted credits, which reduces your CGPA.

Enter your retake marks in the Retake column and this calculator will automatically use the retake score instead of the original marks for that course's grade point.

A CGPA of 3.70+ is Outstanding at NSU. 3.30–3.69 is Excellent, 3.00–3.29 is Very Good, and 2.50–2.99 is Good. The minimum CGPA to remain in good academic standing is generally 2.00.
