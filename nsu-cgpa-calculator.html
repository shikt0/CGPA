About NSU CGPA

North South University (NSU) uses a credit-based grading system where each course has a specific number of credits and corresponding marks. This premium SaaS-style calculator allows students to enter courses, credits, marks, and retake marks, automatically calculating grade points, letter grades, earned credits, and fail warnings.

With retake support, the calculator automatically considers the higher grade for accurate CGPA calculation. It is fully responsive and mobile-friendly, providing a seamless user experience across devices. Users can save their semester using browser storage, ensuring no data loss while planning academics.

The NSU CGPA calculator is ideal for monitoring academic progress, planning course loads, and ensuring students meet graduation requirements efficiently. It supports the standard NSU grading scale, including A, A-, B+, B, B-, C+, C, D, and F grades.

Students are encouraged to use this tool for semester-wise tracking, retake planning, and performance monitoring. The interface highlights failed courses, shows total earned credits, and provides instant CGPA calculation, saving time and minimizing errors.

Use this tool regularly to make informed academic decisions, improve grades, and monitor your overall academic standing. It is designed with a modern SaaS look and feel to provide a premium experience for NSU students.

FAQ

  • How is CGPA calculated at NSU? Multiply each course credit by its grade point, sum them, and divide by total credits.
  • Can I input retake marks? Yes, the higher grade is automatically considered.
  • How does the fail warning work? Courses with F grade display a warning message.
  • Can I save my semester? Yes, the data is stored in your browser.
  • Does this show earned credits? Yes, total earned credits are displayed.
