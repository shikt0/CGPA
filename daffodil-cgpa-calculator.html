About Daffodil International University CGPA

Daffodil International University uses a 4.0 GPA scale with grades A, B, C, D, and F. Students can input marks and credits to calculate CGPA. Retakes are considered automatically to give the highest possible grade.

The interface is fully responsive, works on mobile and desktop, and supports saving semester data in local storage.

Letter grades are displayed beside marks. Fail warnings appear when any course has an F. Earned credits are calculated accurately.

This premium SaaS-style calculator allows Daffodil students to track progress, plan semesters, and meet graduation requirements efficiently.

FAQ

  • How is CGPA calculated? Multiply credit by grade point for each course, sum, then divide by total credits.
  • Can retakes be entered? Yes, calculator chooses higher grade automatically.
  • Does it show earned credits? Yes.
  • Can semester data be saved? Yes, using browser storage.
  • How are fail courses handled? Fail warnings appear immediately.
