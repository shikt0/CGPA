BRAC University CGPA Calculator – Bangladesh

This free BRAC University CGPA Calculator is built specifically for students of BRAC University (BRACU), one of Bangladesh's most prestigious private universities. It uses BRAC University's official grading scale — from A+ (4.00) down to D− (0.70) — to calculate your semester GPA and cumulative CGPA instantly and accurately.

How to Use This Calculator

  • Enter your course name (optional), credit hours, and marks in the fields above.
  • If you have a retake, enter those marks in the Retake column — the calculator uses them automatically.
  • Click Add Course to add more courses, or click Calculate CGPA directly.
  • Your CGPA, earned credits, and any failed course warnings appear instantly in the result section below.
  • Use Save Semester to store your courses in the browser for future use.

BRAC University Grading System Explained

BRAC University uses a detailed 13-level letter grade system on a 4.0 scale. Unlike many universities, BRAC's scale goes all the way down to D− (0.70) for marks of 50–51, meaning students who score 50 or above are not considered failed. Any marks below 50 result in an F (0.00 — Fail).

Both A+ and A carry the same grade point of 4.00 at BRAC University — A+ is awarded for 97–100 marks (Exceptional) while A covers 90–96 (Excellent). Failed courses count toward total attempted credits but receive zero grade points, which brings down your CGPA.
