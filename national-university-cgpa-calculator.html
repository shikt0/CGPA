National University CGPA Calculator – Bangladesh

This free National University CGPA Calculator is designed for students affiliated with National University Bangladesh (NU) — the largest university in Bangladesh by student enrollment, overseeing hundreds of affiliated colleges across every district of the country. It uses NU's official grading scale to calculate your semester GPA and cumulative CGPA instantly and accurately.

How to Use This Calculator

  • Enter your course name (optional), credit hours, and marks in the fields above.
  • If you have a retake or grade improvement, enter those marks in the Retake column — the calculator uses them automatically.
  • Click Add Course to add more courses, or click Calculate CGPA directly.
  • Your CGPA, earned credits, and any failed course warnings appear instantly in the result section below.
  • Use Save Semester to store your course data in the browser for future reference.

National University Grading System Explained

National University Bangladesh uses a 10-level letter grade system ranging from A+ (4.00) for marks of 80–100, down to F (0.00 — Fail) for marks below 40. Unlike many private universities where the pass mark is 50, NU's minimum passing grade is D (2.00) for marks of 40–44.

National University serves millions of students through Honours, Masters, Degree Pass, and professional programs across affiliated colleges nationwide. Failed courses count toward total attempted credits but receive zero grade points, which lowers your CGPA.
