How to Calculate Your CGPA in Bangladesh

CGPA in Bangladesh universities is calculated by multiplying each course's grade point by its credit hours, summing all those values, then dividing by the total credit hours attempted. Different universities — public like National University and private like NSU or BRAC — use different grading scales, which is why university-specific calculators like BDCGPA give more accurate results than generic calculators.
